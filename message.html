Key to Life’s Questions

The first day of class in fourth grade, the textbooks were being handed out. I flipped through my math book to see what fun things I would be learning that year. The book looked different this year than last year’s book. There was red print after each of the problems and test questions. I quickly realized I had the teacher’s key! Pretty exciting for a 9-year-old. I had the answer to every math question that would be asked of me that year. After some pondering, I dutifully returned it to my teacher.
It is not often in life you get the teacher’s key. That you have access to all the answers. Next week we will hear from 15 men who hold the keys of the kingdom. Each of them a special witness to the Savior Jesus Christ. This Saturday and Sunday take the opportunity to listen to what they have to say. Take notes. Ponder their message. And when they give you a challenge or a promise write that down and take the steps to accept and do that challenge or to make that promise effective in your life.
Your answers will not always come to you in bold red font, but if you listen to the words of the prophets and the Spirit, they will come.
Let's enjoy conference together
